@moonseonsaeng_
Hi! I am Nickzel, a ninth-grade student in Philippine Science High School-Main Campus. Being a student is quite challenging, especially when it comes to balancing my academics and extracurriculars. I enjoy studying, but sometimes, things can get exhausting. I did not recognize my feelings at first and even prioritize my studies over my health, especially my mental health. I made this website to tell you that your feelings matter, and you should prioritize yourself no matter what. Being not okay is completely fine, and remember that I am here for you.

I also aim to debunk misconceptions about mental health. I want to stress that our mental well-being is just as important as our physical and emotional health, and discussions about mental health should be normalized. And that's why I added the stigma on mental health and how one can take care of themself and cope or help others get through with the challenges of everyday life, especially during this time of the pandemic.

UsaPangkaisipan is a one-stop website for everything you want and need to know about mental well-being. The website aims to provide accessible, fact-checked, and comprehensive knowledge, debunk misconceptions, and present relevant information revolving around mental health and mental health conditions in the Philippine context.

Let's learn together and be MINDful. Pag-usapan natin 'yan. Importante ka.
